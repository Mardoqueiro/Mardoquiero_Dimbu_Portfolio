About Me!

Developer

I'm Mardoquéiro Dimbu, a passionate coder on an inspiring journey. After matriculating from Wynberg Boys High School in 2023, I delved into the world of computers and coding, fueled by my upbringing in a children's home. It was during the pandemic that I truly discovered my love for coding. In 2022, a career intervention at Specno, a software development company, further ignited my passion. Now, I'm dedicated to enhancing my coding skills at Life Choices Coding Academy, driven by my unwavering love for technology and my ambition to excel in the field.
